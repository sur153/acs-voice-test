# TeleLife Executive Interview

**Audience:** VP, TeleLife Operations (Protective Life)\
**Duration:** 60 minutes\
**Purpose:** Inform **Track 1 (Conversational AI PoC)** and **Track 2
(Executive funding & scale narrative)**

## 1. TeleLife Mission & Value

**Goal:** Anchor the conversation in business reality and executive
priorities.

\*Get walkthrough of Telelife process, inputs & outputs\*

1.  How do you define the core mission of TeleLife within Protective
    today?
2.  Where does TeleLife create the most value in the policy lifecycle?
3.  When leadership evaluates TeleLife performance, which one or two
    outcomes matter most?

## 2. Pressure, Scale & What Breaks

**Goal:** Surface scalability constraints and why the status quo will
not hold.

4.  Where do you feel the most pressure today---cost, speed, quality,
    experience, or risk?
5.  When volume or complexity increases, what tends to break or slow
    down first?
6.  Which inefficiencies are well-known internally but difficult to fix?

## 3. Conversation & Decision Reality

**Goal:** Identify high-value conversational and decision-support
opportunities.

7.  What types of conversations make up the majority of TeleLife
    interactions?
8.  Which conversations are the most judgment-intensive or
    experience-dependent?
9.  What decisions do associates make most frequently during these
    conversations?
10. Where do associates rely most on tribal knowledge or tenure to get
    things right?

## 4. Friction, Knowledge Gaps & Assistance

**Goal:** Pinpoint where Conversational AI could realistically augment
human work.

11. What information is hardest for associates to access in the moment
    of a conversation?
12. Where do conversations most often lead to follow-ups, rework, or
    clarification calls?
13. Where could real-time guidance improve decision quality or
    consistency?

## 5. PoC Focus & Credibility 

**Goal:** Define what an *executive-believable* PoC looks like.

\*15-20 questions that she'd want the PoC to hit\*

14. What single scenario would best illustrate value without requiring
    system integration?
15. What would make a Conversational AI PoC feel realistic rather than
    demo-only?
16. What would you want the PoC to prove or de-risk for you as an
    operator?

scenarios - A clear executive value lens - Explicit success and kill
